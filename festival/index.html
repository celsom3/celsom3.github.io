About Us

The Festival de los Niños will be a day full of entertainment for the whole family to enjoy.

The free event will be held at Kidd Spring Park and Recreation Center (Dallas), and will include; live entertainment, clowns, magicians and bounce houses, prizes, arts and crafts, fashion show, soccer clinics, and educational booths. We will show our children that they are special and important!

Additionally, we aim to empower the Dallas-Fort Worth Latino families providing them with the resources necessary to manage their daily lives and strengthen their family.

There will be a focus on community resources, service providers and businesses such as Legal/Financial advice, Health screenings, and Education opportunities.

Certified coaches will perform ProChallenge Soccer Clinics. Inflatable soccer field and goal will bring extra entertainment. Children will be provided with plenty to drink.

Health Screenings

Dallas County and Parkland hospital will provide childhood immunizations as well as other screenings for the whole family and educational information.

Presentations and demonstrations from resources for the whole family to learn and ask questions on Wellness, Immigration, Finance and more.
