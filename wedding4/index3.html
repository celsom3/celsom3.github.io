United by Tough Times

As undocumented youth, there was a special type of pain we had to risk going through simply by living our lives. Ileana experienced that first hand when she was unconstitutionally arrested set into deportation proceedings during a weekend camping trip near Payson, Ariozna.

Celso was working as a farm hand in Colorado at the time. He had his own undocu-problems of his own to resolve.

Throughout this time, both kept in constant communication and gave each other the strength they both thought to lack.
